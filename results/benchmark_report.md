## Executive Summary

This benchmark report provides a comprehensive strategic analysis for **Groupe Bimo's** new Cookies Banane-Coco-Caramel campaign launch. The analysis covers brand audit, competitive landscape, market positioning, gaps and opportunities, and a detailed target persona — forming the strategic foundation for the creative direction and asset production phases.

### Market Context

The FMCG biscuit category in **North Africa** is characterized by:

- **High fragmentation** — numerous local and regional players competing on price and distribution
- **Global competition** — multinational brands (Oreo, LU, McVitie's) with strong marketing budgets
- **Price sensitivity** — consumers balance quality with affordability
- **Heritage value** — local brands leverage tradition and trust
- **Growing premium segment** — rising middle class seeking indulgent experiences

### Digital Presence Assessment

| Channel | Current State | Opportunity |
|:--------|:--------------|:------------|
| **Website** | Functional but dated — basic product listings, no lifestyle imagery | Transform into immersive brand experience with visual storytelling |
| **Social Media** | Limited presence — sporadic posts, low engagement | Create consistent content calendar with high-quality visuals |
| **OOH / Billboard** | Not currently utilized | Ready-to-use billboard mockups for outdoor placements |
| **E-commerce** | Not visible | Product photography assets for online retail partners |

### Campaign Need

Launch photography and billboard mockups for the new Cookies Banane-Coco-Caramel line, emphasizing **authentic real-life visuals** over artificial renders. The client requires high-quality visual assets that communicate product quality, flavor innovation, and everyday indulgence.
