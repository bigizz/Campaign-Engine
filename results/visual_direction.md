## Moodboards: Conceptual Themes

#### Lighting Direction
- **Style:** Natural light, golden hour
- **Source:** Window light from camera left
- **Quality:** Soft, diffused with warm color temperature (~3200K)

#### Color Temperature
- **Overall:** Warm (amber/golden tones)
- **Accent:** Warm whites and cream tones
- **Pop:** Banana yellow for energy

### Color Roles & Usage

| Color | Role | Usage | Hex |
|:------|:-----|:------|:----|
| **Cocoa Brown** | Primary — foundation | Headlines, backgrounds, text | `#3D2314` |
| **Caramel Gold** | Primary accent | Highlights, call-to-action, decorative elements | `#D49A36` |
| **Creamy Off-White** | Background | Page backgrounds, card surfaces, negative space | `#F9F6F0` |

### Color Psychology

| Color | Emotional Association | Brand Application |
|:------|:----------------------|:------------------|
| Cocoa Brown | Warmth, stability, richness | Grounds the brand in cocoa heritage and quality |
| Caramel Gold | Premium, appetizing, warmth | Elevates products with a premium feel |
| Creamy Off-White | Clean, natural, airy | Creates breathing room and lets products shine |
| Banana Yellow | Energy, happiness, playfulness | Communicates flavor innovation and joy |
| Fresh Mint | Freshness, natural, calm | Reinforces natural ingredients and balance |

## Typography

<div style="border: 1px solid #D49A36; border-radius: 16px; padding: 24px; margin: 24px 0; background: #F9F6F0;">

### Font Pairing

| Usage | Font | Weight | Size Range | Example |
|:------|:-----|:-------|:-----------|:--------|
| **Headlines / Billboard** | Outfit | Bold (700) | 36–72px | <span style="font-family: 'Outfit', sans-serif; font-weight: 700; font-size: 28px; color: #3D2314;">Nouveau Cookies</span> |
| **Subheadings** | Outfit | SemiBold (600) | 20–32px | <span style="font-family: 'Outfit', sans-serif; font-weight: 600; font-size: 20px; color: #D49A36;">Banane · Coco · Caramel</span> |
| **Body Copy** | Inter | Regular (400) | 14–18px | <span style="font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 400; font-size: 16px; color: #3D2314;">Le plaisir authentique au quotidien</span> |
| **Accent / Data** | Inter | Medium (500) | 12–16px | <span style="font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 500; font-size: 14px; color: #D49A36;">GROUPE BIMO · SINCE 1981</span> |

### Typography Guidelines

- **Headlines:** Use Outfit Bold for maximum impact in billboard layouts and hero sections. Letter-spacing: -0.5px for compact headlines.
- **Subheadings:** Outfit SemiBold with letter-spacing: +1px for a refined, premium feel.
- **Body:** Inter Regular at 16px for comfortable reading. Line-height: 1.6.
- **Accents:** Inter Medium in Caramel Gold for data points, statistics, and brand highlights.
- **Hierarchy:** Clear visual hierarchy from headlines → subheadings → body → accents.

### Font Licensing
Outfit and Inter are open-source fonts available via Google Fonts, free for commercial use.

## Style Guidelines

### Imagery Style

| Attribute | Standard | Billboard Variant |
|:----------|:---------|:------------------|
| **Style** | Photorealistic, "slice-of-life" | High-contrast, dramatic |
| **Lighting** | Natural — golden hour or bright morning window light | Studio lighting with dramatic shadows |
| **Composition** | Macro shots emphasizing textures | Bold product hero with minimal background |
| **Color Treatment** | Warm, inviting tones | Saturated, high-impact colors |
| **Depth of Field** | Shallow (f/2.8) for intimate feel | Deep (f/8+) for full product clarity |
| **Textures** | Chocolate chips, caramel drizzle, coconut flakes visible | Smooth, clean product surface |

### Layout Rules

| Medium | Layout | Key Elements |
|:-------|:-------|:-------------|
| **Billboard** | Centered product + headline | Logo bottom-right, tagline below |
| **Digital / Web** | Large hero image with overlapping typography | Navigation overlay, CTA button |
| **Social Media** | Square crop (1:1) with product focus | Brand frame, minimal text |
| **Print** | Full-bleed imagery with typography overlay | Color bar, QR code, legal text |

### Tone

**Warm but not sentimental — confident and modern.**

The visual tone should feel approachable and premium simultaneously. Avoid artificial, overly polished aesthetics. Embrace natural textures, real environments, and genuine moments.
