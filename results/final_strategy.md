# Executive Summary

<div style="border-left: 4px solid #D49A36; padding-left: 24px; margin: 24px 0;">

## Campaign Objective

Launch a visually compelling campaign for **Groupe Bimo's new Cookies Banane-Coco-Caramel** product line, supported by high-quality lifestyle photography, billboard mockups, and brand identity assets that communicate authentic indulgence and modern appeal.

</div>

### Key Findings

| Area | Finding |
|:-----|:--------|
| **Brand Position** | Established local heritage brand with opportunity to modernize visual presence |
| **Market Gap** | Dated digital presence with no lifestyle imagery or OOH advertising |
| **Target Audience** | "Everyday Indulgers" — 18–45, value quality + convenience, seek authentic visuals |
| **Creative Direction** | Two themes: "Authentic Indulgence" (warm, natural, slice-of-life) and "Urban Snack Moment" (bold, energetic, city-centric) |
| **Asset Delivery** | 6 SVG brand assets + 1 PNG lifestyle render, all with full specification documentation |

### Deliverables Produced

| Phase | Deliverable | Key Output |
|:------|:------------|:-----------|
| **1. Strategist** | `benchmark_report.md` | Brand audit, competitive landscape, SWOT, persona |
| **2. Creative Director** | `visual_direction.md` | Moodboards, color palette, typography, style guidelines |
| **3. Designer** | `media_kit.md` + assets | 6 visual renders with full spec sheets |
| **4. Consolidation** | `final_strategy.md` + `final_strategy.pdf` | This document — a complete visual campaign strategy |

</div>

# Phase 2: Visual Direction — Creative Guidelines

## Moodboards

### Theme 1: "Authentic Indulgence" (Primary)

<div style="border: 1px solid #D49A36; border-radius: 12px; padding: 20px; margin: 16px 0; background: #F9F6F0;">

<div style="display: flex; gap: 12px; flex-wrap: wrap; margin: 12px 0;">
  <span style="background: #3D2314; color: #F9F6F0; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Warm</span>
  <span style="background: #D49A36; color: #FFF; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Inviting</span>
  <span style="background: #F4D03F; color: #3D2314; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Visceral</span>
  <span style="background: #A3D9A5; color: #3D2314; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Natural</span>
</div>

**Vision:** Real-world scenarios — sunlight across a rustic wooden table, a hand breaking a cookie in half, crumbs catching light, steam rising from coffee beside the package.

**Lighting:** Natural golden hour light, soft and diffused (~3200K).  
**Composition:** Macro shots emphasizing textures — chocolate chips, caramel drizzle, coconut flakes.

</div>

### Theme 2: "Urban Snack Moment" (Billboard Focus)

<div style="border: 1px solid #D49A36; border-radius: 12px; padding: 20px; margin: 16px 0; background: #F9F6F0;">

<div style="display: flex; gap: 12px; flex-wrap: wrap; margin: 12px 0;">
  <span style="background: #2C3E50; color: #FFF; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Bold</span>
  <span style="background: #D49A36; color: #FFF; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Energetic</span>
  <span style="background: #F4D03F; color: #3D2314; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Modern</span>
  <span style="background: #3D2314; color: #F9F6F0; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 16px; font-size: 13px;">Urban</span>
</div>

**Vision:** Bold outdoor scenes — cookie against sunlit city skyline, street-side café table, oversized product with typography over urban backdrop.

**Readability:** Large typography visible from 50m distance.  
**Background:** City skyline with soft blur. Product oversized with dramatic scale.

</div>
